# Our Recruitment Process

We follow a structured, transparent process designed to deliver the right talent efficiently and reliably.

1. **Client Consultation** — Understanding your business objectives and workforce needs.
2. **Job Requirement Analysis** — Defining the role, skills, and eligibility criteria.
3. **Candidate Sourcing** — Tapping into our talent networks and sourcing channels.
4. **Screening & Assessment** — Structured evaluation of qualifications, skills, and experience.
5. **Interview Management** — Coordinating and managing the interview process.
6. **Reference Verification** — Validating candidate background and credentials.
7. **Offer Negotiation** — Supporting fair and transparent offer discussions.
8. **Visa & Relocation Support** — Assisting with documentation, compliance, and relocation logistics.
9. **Onboarding Assistance** — Ensuring a smooth transition into the new role.
